Pro Tips

  • Be specific: Mention exact colors or moods (e.g., "forest green", "energetic")
  • Describe context: Industry, audience, or purpose helps AI choose better colors
  • Reference examples: "Like Apple's minimalism" or "Spotify's bold style"
  • Mention constraints: "Must be accessible" or "needs high contrast"
